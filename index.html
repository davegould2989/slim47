Slim 47 are a 4 piece, indie/rock covers band based in Northampton. We play anthemic tracks from bands like Foo Fighters, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Oasis, Blur, Kings of Leon and Killers to name just a few.

We have built a reputation as being one of the best covers bands around. With our full on set consisting of indie/rock anthems from the 90's & 2000's, coupled with a confidence in front of any size crowd, we always keep the venue busy.

We get booked to play at all sorts of venues across the region. Regulars on the pub circuit and at showcase gigs at events held at Santa Pod, Le Mans, London Marathon and festivals of all sizes, we are always in demand and also perform at private functions, parties and weddings.
